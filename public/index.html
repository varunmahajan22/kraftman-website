About Kraftman

A Decade of Packaging Excellence

Kraftman Packaging is a leading paperboard packaging manufacturer specializing in customized solutions for FMCG, retail, and consumer goods industries.

Established in 2016 and headquartered in Faridabad, Haryana, we operate a state-of-the-art facility equipped with Heidelberg multicolor offset presses, CTP technology, and automated finishing lines — delivering sustainable packaging that meets international quality standards.

Our expertise spans mono cartons, corrugated packaging, hangtags, display boxes, and commercial printing. We combine advanced manufacturing with rigorous quality control to ensure product protection, brand enhancement, and supply chain efficiency.
